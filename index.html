Hi there, I'm Camilla Scolini!

Welcome to my webpage! I am a NASA Jack Eddy postdoctoral fellow at the Institute for the Study of Earth, Oceans, and Space at the University of New Hampshire. My research focuses on solar eruptions, particularly Coronal Mass Ejections and the study of their “effectiveness” – their ability to affect planetary systems in different ways.
