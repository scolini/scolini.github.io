Welcome to my new personal homepage!

I am a NASA Jack Eddy postdoctoral fellow at the Institute for the Study of Earth, Oceans, and Space at the University of New Hampshire. My research focuses on solar eruptions, particularly Coronal Mass Ejections and the study of their “effectiveness” – their ability to affect planetary systems and human activities in space in different ways.

To learn more about my research interests, click on the research tab. You can find my CV here, and my full list of publications here.
